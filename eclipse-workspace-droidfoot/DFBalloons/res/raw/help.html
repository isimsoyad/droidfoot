DFBalloons

Standard-Greenfoot-Scenario (www.greenfoot.org) ported to Android (see project Droidfoot, http://www.programmierkurs-java.de/droidfoot)

Rules

Pop the balloons by touching. Drag a bomb on top of a balloon to make an explosion that will wipe out many balloons at once.

Menu-Items

  • Change speed (By touching the icon there will appear a slider, who will disappear by touching the home-icon)
  • Start or resume / pause
  • Reset (= new game)
  • Help
  • Settings (scaling, screen orientation)

Icons

  • Speed-Icon: http://www.visualpharm.com/
  • Reset-Icon: http://www.endlessicons.com/free-icons/reset-icon/
